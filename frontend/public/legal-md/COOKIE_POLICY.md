## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide a better user experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and improve our services.

At Oshocks Junior Bike Shop, we use cookies to enhance your shopping experience, process your orders securely, remember your cart items, and provide personalized product recommendations. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

> **Your Control:** You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Please note that removing or blocking cookies can impact your user experience and may prevent certain features from working properly.

---

## Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize cookies based on their purpose and functionality.

### Essential Cookies
- **session_id** - Maintains your login session and shopping cart (Session, Oshocks)
- **csrf_token** - Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks (24 hours, Oshocks)
- **cart_token** - Stores your shopping cart items (7 days, Oshocks)
- **auth_token** - Authenticates your user account (30 days, Oshocks)
- **cookie_consent** - Remembers your cookie preferences (1 year, Oshocks)

### Functional Cookies
- **language_pref** - Remembers your language preference (1 year, Oshocks)
- **currency_pref** - Stores your preferred currency (KES/USD) (1 year, Oshocks)
- **location_city** - Remembers your delivery location for faster checkout (90 days, Oshocks)
- **recently_viewed** - Tracks recently viewed products for your convenience (30 days, Oshocks)
- **wishlist_items** - Saves your wishlist items (1 year, Oshocks)

### Analytics Cookies
- **_ga** - Google Analytics - distinguishes users (2 years, Google)
- **_gid** - Google Analytics - distinguishes users (24 hours, Google)
- **_gat** - Google Analytics - throttles request rate (1 minute, Google)
- **algolia_analytics** - Tracks product search behavior (6 months, Algolia)
- **session_recording** - Records user interactions for improvement (Session, Oshocks)

### Marketing Cookies
- **_fbp** - Facebook Pixel - tracks conversions and retargeting (3 months, Facebook)
- **IDE** - Google Ads - serves targeted advertisements (1 year, Google)
- **fr** - Facebook advertising delivery and metrics (3 months, Facebook)
- **personalization_id** - Twitter - personalizes ads and content (2 years, Twitter)
- **affiliate_ref** - Tracks affiliate marketing referrals (30 days, Oshocks)

### Payment Processing Cookies
- **stripe_mid** - Stripe fraud detection and processing (1 year, Stripe)
- **stripe_sid** - Stripe session management (30 minutes, Stripe)
- **mpesa_session** - M-Pesa payment session tracking (Session, Safaricom)
- **flutterwave_ref** - Flutterwave transaction reference (24 hours, Flutterwave)

### Customer Support Cookies
- **tawk_uuid** - Tawk.to - identifies returning chat users (6 months, Tawk.to)
- **tawk_visitor** - Tawk.to - tracks visitor information (6 months, Tawk.to)
- **support_history** - Stores your support ticket history (1 year, Oshocks)

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences through our Cookie Consent Manager, which appears when you first visit our website. You can also change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer.

> **Important Note:** Please note that blocking essential cookies will prevent certain features from working properly. You may not be able to add items to your cart, complete purchases, or access your account if essential cookies are disabled.

### Browser Cookie Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to reject cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

- **Google Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Mozilla Firefox:** Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
- **Microsoft Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
- **Opera:** Settings → Privacy & security → Cookies

For more information about managing cookies in your specific browser, please visit your browser's help section or support website.

## Cookies in Mobile Applications

Our mobile applications may use technologies similar to cookies, including mobile identifiers, SDKs, and local storage. These technologies serve similar purposes to web cookies, helping us provide a personalized shopping experience on mobile devices.

You can manage mobile tracking through your device settings. On iOS, go to Settings → Privacy → Tracking. On Android, go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
