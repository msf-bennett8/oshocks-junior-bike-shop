## 1. Shipping Overview

At Oshocks Junior Bike Shop, we're committed to delivering your cycling products safely and efficiently across Kenya. This Shipping Policy outlines our delivery methods, timeframes, costs, and what you can expect when ordering from us.

### What We Offer
- ✅ **Nationwide Delivery** - We ship to all major cities and rural areas in Kenya
- ✅ **Fast Processing** - Orders processed within 24 hours on business days
- ✅ **Secure Packaging** - Professional packaging to protect your items
- ✅ **Real-Time Tracking** - Track your order from dispatch to delivery

> **Free Shipping Available!** Enjoy **FREE standard shipping** on orders over **KES 10,000** within Nairobi (CBD and Suburbs). Offer applies to standard delivery only. Express delivery charges still apply.

> **Important Notice:** Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to factors beyond our control, including weather conditions, courier delays, public holidays, or security situations. We'll keep you informed of any delays.
